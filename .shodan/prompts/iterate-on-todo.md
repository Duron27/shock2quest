description: "Groom todo list; implement or plan"

Review the items remaining in the TODO.md at the root of the repo. Think through what can be pulled from that list. Note that these are unpolished items and may require planning and thinking ahead of time to

You may do one of the following, in priority order:

1. If there is a small, scoped, unfinished task with clear deliverables - go ahead and implement it.
2. If there is a larger task, you may create a project plan for it, follow the convention of the projects/ folder. Make sure to thoroughly research and break up the plan into simple phases with clear, scoped deliverables at each point.

In either case, create a new branch for your change, and open a PR with the changes for review.
